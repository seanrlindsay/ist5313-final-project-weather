Tornado - Basic Facts

  • Can appear without warning as funnel-shaped clouds extending down from a thunderstorm with swirling winds up to 300 mph and a damage path up to a mile wide
  • Flying debris causes the most deaths from a tornado
  • Make a loud roaring sound, similar to a freight train
  • Have an average forward speed of 30 mph, but can move up to 70 mph
  • When formed over water they are known as waterspouts
