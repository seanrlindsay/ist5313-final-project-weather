Severe Thunderstorms & Lightning - Basic Facts

  • Lightning kills 51 Americans and causes debilitating injuries to hundreds more
  • Flash floods from storms cause more than 140 U.S. deaths each year
  • Thunderstorms can produce heavy rain fall for up to a full hour
  • Lightning may strike as much as 10 miles away from the rain
  • The average chance of being struck by lightning is 1 in 600,000
  • Thunder is the sound made as the lightning bolt super heats the air around it so that it expands and contracts quickly, like a hand clap
  • “Heat lightning” is the term for lightning in a storm too far away to hear the thunder
  • Dangers include flash floods, lightning, strong winds, hail, tornadoes and wild fires sparked by lightning
