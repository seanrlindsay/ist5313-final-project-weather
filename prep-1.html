Preparedness - Overview

Be prepared
  • Know when severe weather is possible by paying attention to local weather forecasts
  • Get the items you will need ahead of time and keep them easily accessible
  • Update your Emergency Preparedness Kit every 6 months to make sure things haven't expired

Stay Safe
  • Find the safest place possible to wait out the weather
  • Help others especially the elderly, pets, and small children
  • Remain calm and spend quality time with your loved ones
  • If asked to evacuate, do so immediately and drive safely
  • Keep yourself updated by continuing to monitor local weather and news
