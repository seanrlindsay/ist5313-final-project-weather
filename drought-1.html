Drought & Extreme Heat - Basic Facts

  • A Heat Wave is an extended period of extreme heat with high humidity
  • An Excessive Heat Warning warns of daytime highs of 105 °F - 110 °F for at least 2 days
  • A Drought is a long period of low rainfall causing crop damage and can last for years
